// 01 — About Me

Engineering at the Intersection of Hardware & Software

I am an Electronic and Computer Engineer with hands-on experience spanning embedded systems design, backend software development, and intelligent automation platforms. My foundation was built at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT), where I trained in electronics, control theory, and software engineering.

My engineering philosophy centres on integrated systems thinking — understanding how physical infrastructure connects with modern software to create solutions that are reliable, scalable, and impactful. From PCB layout and circuit schematics to secure web platforms and IoT-inspired monitoring systems, I approach every challenge from a full-system perspective.

Through professional engagements at Kenya Power and Lighting Company, Fedha Electrics, and JHUB Africa, I have built practical competencies across electrical distribution, embedded electronics, and agile software development — giving me a uniquely broad foundation across the engineering landscape.
